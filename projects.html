My Projects

Generative AI Project

For this project, I am working with VEO 3, a generative AI service by Google, to create a unique video of Reveille X and her eye patch. I used a Meme from the Metal Gear Solid community, with an edit of the song "Invisible" by Duran Duran, to add to the AI-generated video. The AI-generated video below shows one of the project's results so far, before I reached the limit of generation.

We are currently focused on creating a proper prompt to get the best results for the video as possible, with what we have below as our current results before the limit.

  • GENERATE: A high-fidelity, photorealistic video of a collie dog wearing a tactical black eye-patch over its right eye.
  • SCENE: The dog walks slowly and deliberately down a long, metallic, industrial hallway in a decommissioned military installation. The lighting is dark and dusty, illuminated only by a slow, intermittent red emergency light flashing from the ceiling.
  • MOTION: The dog is in a low-angle tracking shot, walking at a dramatically slow pace. The clip must be generated to seamlessly loop like a GIF.
  • NEGATIVE PROMPT (Things to Exclude): No people, no collar, no leash, no other animals.
